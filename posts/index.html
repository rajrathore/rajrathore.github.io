How Did I Get This Domain?

I needed to make a resume or biodata to give it to someone for the post of becoming a husband..:) yeah, it was a matrimonial biodata and I thought it would be convenient for me as well as for them to view my biodata online instead of opening email everytime to view the biodata… So, I thought of setting up a small website and provide the url to them… and I can make changes to it if they ask for more information…

I did a look up on Godaddy and… Voila!! rajrathore.com and rajrathore.me is available.. wow I didn’t think I’ll get this domain. Raj is a pretty common name in India and there are lots of Rathores… but I guess very few are programmers.. that helped, I guess.

I registered the domain and took a template and filled it up with all the details and uploaded to the server, just in two hours… adding information took time, everything else was snappy.

I never needed a biodata, so I never set it up… and I think, I’m never going to need one except for this one scenario… .. I’ll make a nice well-designed/well-crafted profile someday…

A person’s writing style also tells a lot about a person as well… read my blogs and know my thinking.. see my work and know my skills & spend time with me and know me as a human being… Task simplified!

I’m thinking of writing more often and as it happens with other skills, more time you invest, better you become!

Have a nice day!
