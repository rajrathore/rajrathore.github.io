How I Got This Domain?

I needed to make a matrimonial bio-data and I thought it would be convenient for me as well as for them to view my bio-data online instead of opening email everytime to view my bio-data... So, I thought of setting up a small website and provide the url to them... also It allows me to make changes to it if asked for more information... I did a look up on Godaddy and... **Voila!!** __rajrathore.com__ and __rajrathore.me__ was available. **Raj** is a pretty common name in India and there are lots of **Rathores**... but I guess very few are programmers.. that increased my chances of getting this domain. A domain name with your own name is a really good thing to possess.
