How I Got This Domain?

Apr 23rd, 2014

I needed to make a matrimonial bio-data and I thought it would be convenient for me as well as for them to view my bio-data online instead of opening email everytime to view my bio-data… So, I thought of setting up a small website and provide the url to them… also It allows me to make changes to it if asked for more information…

I did a look up on Godaddy and… Voila!! rajrathore.com and rajrathore.me was available. Raj is a pretty common name in India and there are lots of Rathores… but I guess very few are programmers.. that increased my chances of getting this domain.

A domain name with your own name is a really good thing to possess.

I registered the domain and took a template and filled it up with all the details and uploaded to the server, just in two hours… editing template & adding information took some time, everything else was snappy.

I never needed a bio-data, so I never set it up… and I think, I’m never going to need one except for this one scenario… .. I’ll make a nice well-designed/well-crafted profile someday…

Bio-data is just summary of a person and I think a person’s writing style also tells a lot about a person as well…

Read my blogs and know my thinking,
See my work and know my skills,
Spend time with me and know me as a human being…
Task simplified!!

I’m considering of writing more often and as it happens with other skills, more time you invest, better you become!

Have a nice day!
